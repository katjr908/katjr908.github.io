Chess

Chess is an ancient game in which two players start with sixteen pieces each on a 64 square by 64 square board. The two sides are known as 'Black' and 'White'. The game of chess was created in around the 6th century CE. There are six different types of chess pieces:

  • King
  • Queen
  • Rook
  • Bishop
  • Knight
  • Pawn

Though the game of chess was created centuries ago, chess is still played all over the world.
