Chess Notation

Notation is very important in chess; it is how you learn from your mistakes. Using notation you can replay through your games.

Row Piece Piece Symbol
1 Knight N
2 Bishop B
3 Queen Q
4 King K
5 Rook R
6 Pawn N/A
7 Captures x
8 Check +
9 Checkmate #
10 Castle Kingside 0-0
11 Castle Queenside 0-0-0

For notation piece symbols, you'll notice that most of them is the first letter of the actual piece name. The two exceptions are the knight and the pawn. For the pawn, you would just write the square it lands on. For the knight, you use the N because the K is used for the King. For more on castling visit the Special Moves page.
