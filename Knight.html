The Knight

The Knight is by far the most trickiest piece on the board, they are the only pieces able to jump over other pieces. They look like a horse, but they are called knights, not horses.

Knights move in 'L' shapes. They move up/down two squares, then left/right one square. Or left/right two squares, then up/down one square. They can jump over pieces in its way, but it can only capture the piece on the square it lands on. Below is an image showing a knight and its moves.
