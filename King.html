The King

The king is the most important piece, if the king is attacked, it is called 'check'. If a king is attacked, but every move it can go to is covered, it is checkmate and the game is over. If the king isn't in check, but the king and any of its other pieces can't move, it is a draw. The point of the game is to deliver checkmate. Below is the image af a king:
